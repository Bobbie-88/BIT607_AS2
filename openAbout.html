About Us

The Haukai restaurant is a family owned business, started by Teariki and Aria Wiremu. Their aim is to celebrate traditional Kai and Māori culture in the Bay of Islands.

Teariki and Aria have grown up within the local iwi and have now been together for over 30 years. They have 3 children together, who help out with the business when they can.

They share a passion of showcasing Māori history, traditions and culture and passing them on to the younger generations.
